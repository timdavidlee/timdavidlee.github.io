  • Project: Darwin - machine learning to find similiar article postings

    Problem? There's too much news to read.

    Solution? Create a RSS reading platform powered by machine learning to find the news most relevant to someone. This is established by taking an existing body of articles and finding more news articles similar to that "topic"

    1. Slack Integration with Chatbot features
    2. User - based recommendations
    3. Topic - based recommendations
    4. Optimized model selection depending on low or high volume article sets

  • Project: The NLP language of Job Postings

    Data science and AI is a growing field with many impressive applications, who is doing all this works? Just data scientists? What other jobs have interactions data science? Are there hybrid jobs?

    My Capstone analysis leveraged Natural Language Processing (NLP) with statistical techniques to parse 22k job postings, with a basic premise: can job details predict the job title? Building the model and delving into job postings, interesting insights in the current industry, Data Scientist is very similar to Data Engineers and Data Analysts, and much more.
    The prediction accuracy was ~0.60

  • Python - Modeling Company financials by creating randomized transactional data

    Link to the Python on Github

    Can't design the next hadoop, or the next D3 without data. And if you don't work for a big data company, what can you do? Make it. From my experience in consulting and finance, I programmed a data generator that generates 500k-1M rows that resemble typical SEC 10k and 10Q company filings. I currently can generate balance sheet data in pandas dataframe or CSV format, with arbitrary product detail. Maybe its 2 additional fields of SKU and product group, maybe its 6 with cost_center and project ID. This is all randomize and functionally generated
